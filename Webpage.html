About the Workshop

What You'll Learn

This hands-on workshop is designed for beginners and enthusiasts who want to dive into the world of analog photography. We'll move beyond automatic settings and explore the fundamentals of creating beautiful images on film.

  • Camera Basics: Understanding aperture, shutter speed, and ISO.
  • Film Stocks: Exploring different types of film and their characteristics.
  • Composition: Techniques for creating compelling photographs.
  • Lighting: How to use natural light to your advantage.
  • Developing & Scanning: An introduction to the darkroom and digitization process.

What to Bring

Please bring your own 35mm film camera. If you don't have one, we have a limited number of cameras available for rent for an additional €10. One roll of black and white film is included in the workshop price.
